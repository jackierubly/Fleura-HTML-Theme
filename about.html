Curating luxury aesthetics and brands

Hey friends, I'm Fleura — the girl behind the brand.

Dedicated to classic, cozy-meets-elevated visuals; blending luxe, layered palettes across tactile print, digital, and interior moments. I’ve spent a decade refining a process that feels personal and pressure-free, allowing you to enjoy the creative journey while we build something timeless together.

When not designing, you’ll find me styling coffee-table vignettes, hunting dusty bookshops, and making far too many espressos.

THE JOURNEY

2011

Launched with our first design client — completing a brand

Fresh out of school, we began taking on boutique brand projects and learning how design could elevate small teams, solopreneurs, and local shops.

2016

Moved into our first office space on Main Street

Space for clients, samples, and meetings—plus enough sunlight for plants. We formalized our process and introduced mood boards and proof rounds.

2021

Launched our boutique shop and in‑person planning sessions

We expanded services to include stationery, print collateral, and creative direction; partnered with stylists and photographers to offer full brand shoots.

2025

Expanded to offer 360° services to better serve our clients

From brand identity to interiors—ensuring every touchpoint feels cohesive and considered.
